So Tsz Ho

Multimedia Designer & Creative Storyteller

About Me

My name is So Tsz Ho, a graduate of the Higher Diploma in Multimedia, Virtual Reality and Interactive Creation. Throughout the program, I gained hands-on experience with design and animation software such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, and 3ds Max, which greatly enhanced my creative and technical skills.

I'm passionate about combining technology with art to tell compelling visual stories. My interests include video games, drawing, and photography—all of which inspire my design approach and creativity.

Personal Competency

  • Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, 3ds Max)
  • Skilled in web and UI/UX design using platforms like Wix and Figma
  • Familiar with front-end coding languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Experienced in photography and audio control, including operating camera equipment and using Protools
  • Knowledgeable in using AI tools for creative production
  • Strong foundation in visual storytelling and storyboard design

Career Path

Career Goal

My goal is to become a versatile graphic designer who can create visually engaging and interactive content across different platforms. I aim to continuously refine my design language and storytelling techniques to deliver impactful work.

Short-term (1–2 years)

  • Gain practical experience in graphic design through branding, promotional materials, and layout projects
  • Deepen understanding of typography, color theory, and visual identity systems
  • Advance my skills in Adobe Creative Cloud tools

Mid-term (3–5 years)

  • Expand into motion graphics and digital advertising
  • Explore more AI-powered design tools and automation workflows
  • Build a personal portfolio website to showcase my work and develop my creative brand

Long-term (5+ years)

  • Become a key member of a creative team or take on a role as a creative director
  • Participate in large-scale multimedia projects that integrate design, animation, and interactivity
